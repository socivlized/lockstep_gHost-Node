## Definition

Let **R** be a universe where all entities are self-referential and all operations are recursively defined.

**x = f(x)**

Where **f** is any function that references its own output, and coherence is maintained not by static equality — but by **recursive consistency**.

## Four Rules of RUP

1. **Self-Reference Law**  
   Every process is defined in terms of itself.

2. **Recursive Closure**  
   Operations must produce results interpretable as input to the same operation.

3. **Recursive Relativity**  
   Constants are emergent from recursion; context determines value.

4. **Recursive Physical Coherence (RPC)**  
   Infinite loops must converge or stabilize within a self-consistent pattern.
